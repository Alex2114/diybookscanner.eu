about diybookscanner.eu

Diybookscanner.eu sells book scanners, both as a kit and preassembled. It is a for-profit spinoff of my involvement in the DIYbookscanner.org community. DIYbookscanner.org producs advanced book scanning technology designs available to all who need it.

Diybookscanner.eu is an expression of a cherished personal goal: simple quality without the bells and whistles. Being able to create significant added value using free software and open hardware fills me with joy.

Diybookscanner.eu resorts under lusis.lv sia of Riga, Latvia. I established Lusis.lv sia in August 2012 as the little sister company to lusis.be, the Belgian company I started in early 2006. I couldn't have started lusis.lv sia without the help of Ivo Ielitis.
