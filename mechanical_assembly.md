### Preparing the aluminium tubes
You will need:
* 2x aluminium square tube 30x694mm
* 4x 30x30 M8 tube insert
* rubber hammer

Bang the tube inserts into both sides of both aluminium tubes using the rubber hammer. This requires some force.
