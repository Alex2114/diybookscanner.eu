Diybookscanner.eu, a good community citizen

At diybookscanner.eu, we try really hard to be good community citizens. All the progress we make while producing the diybookscanner gets published under free-as-in-freedom licenses. We proudly share our work. Always. With everyone. We strongly believe that is the right thing to do.

When you buy our product, you don't buy crippleware. Our product doesn't die of planned obsolescense. If anything, our product will grow in possibilities.

Some of our contributions:

  • Canon A2200 electronical triggering script: http://github.com/markvdb/diybookscanner/misc/test_keypedal.sh
  • openscad porting work: http://github.com/markvdb/diybookscanner/openscad (in progress, helping hands welcome!)
  • mechanical improvement: aluminium L profiles as brackets for the glass
  • documenting Canon A2200 ptp triggering: http://github.com/markvdb/diybookscanner/misc/settings.md
  • ptpcam debian packaging (work in progress)
  • ... and more to come!
