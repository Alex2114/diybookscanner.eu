Frequently asked questions

Products & pricing

Is your kit available now?
Yes it is.

Do you offer bulk pricing?
Yes, we can offer bulk pricing for quantities of five or more kits. Please contact info@diybookscanner.eu for more information about this.

Do you offer discounts to non-profits?
No, unfortunately, we can't offer any lower price. We are open to suggestions on how to make it easier for non-profits to buy our products. This might include sponsorship, group buy, pooling of donations. Please don't hesitate to contact info@diybookscanner.eu if you have more suggestions.

I am only interested in part of the kit. Can I order separate parts?
Sorry, no.

I am only interested in the parts, without the remote video assistance service. Can I order them separately?
No, unfortunately, both come together as a package.

Payment

What forms of payment do you accept?
At the moment, we only accept wire transfer (SEPA payment) in euros.

Do you accept online payment by credit card?
Not yet, but we are working on it. Please subscribe to our newsletter if you would like to be informed when this or other functionality becomes available.

Shipping

How much does shipping cost?
Shipping within the EU is included in the price.

Where do you ship?
We ship within the EU, Switzerland and Norway. We are willing to examine if we can sell to other countries cost effectively too. Please contact info@diybookscanner.eu to work that out before ordering.
We do not ship to the Americas, our US partner does. See diybookscanner.org for more information.

Is your package insured?
Yes, of course it is. Our basic kit is insured up to 500€.
Please note that if we send glass, we do so on a best effort basis only. We do our very best to package it carefully, but if it's broken on arrival, we will not send you a replacement. 3mm float glass is cheap (8€ or less) and easy to source locally.

How fast do you ship?
For standard shipping, expect about one week between our receipt of your payment and the courier ringing at your door. Extra fast shipping is possible at a premium. Please contact info@diybookscanner.eu for more information about this.

More shipping questions?
Please contact us directly by email.

What is your relationship to diybookscanner.org?
We produce the diybookscanner.org design for the EU market. We try to be a good community citizen by actively contributing to the diybookscanner.org project in several ways. Some examples:

  • Canon A2200 electronical triggering script: http://github.com/markvdb/diybookscanner/misc/test_keypedal.sh
  • openscad porting work: http://github.com/markvdb/diybookscanner/openscad (in progress, helping hands welcome!)
  • an experiment with aluminium L profiles as brackets for the glass
  • documenting Canon A2200 ptp triggering: http://github.com/markvdb/diybookscanner/misc/settings.md
  • ptpcam debian packaging (work in progress)
