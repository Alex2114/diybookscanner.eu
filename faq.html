Frequently asked questions

Products & pricing

Is your kit available now?
Yes. The five first orders will be shipped out before 2012/09/01, if not sooner.

Do you offer bulk pricing?
Yes, we can offer bulk pricing for quantities of five or more kits. Please contact info@diybookscanner.eu for more information about this.

Do you offer discounts to non-profits?
No, but we are open to suggestions on how to make it easier for non-profits to buy our products. Some ideas have already been submitted and we are working on them.

I am only interested in part of the kit. Can I order separate parts?
The wooden parts are available separately, but other parts are not. Please contact info@diybookscanner.eu for more information about this.

Shipping

How much does shipping cost?
Shipping within the EU is included in the price.

Where do you ship?
We ship within the EU. We are willing to examine if we can do Switzerland and Norway cost effectively too. Please contact info@diybookscanner.eu to work that out before ordering.
We do not ship to the Americas, our US partner does. See diybookscanner.org for more information.

Is your package insured?
Yes, of course it is. Our basic kit is insured up to 500€.

How fast do you ship?
For standard shipping, expect about one week between our receipt of your payment and the courier ringing at your door. Extra fast shipping is possible at a premium. Please contact info@diybookscanner.eu for more information about this.

More shipping questions?
Please contact us directly by email.

What is your relationship to diybookscanner.org?
We produce the diybookscanner.org design for the EU market. We try to be a good community citizen by actively contributing to the diybookscanner.org project in several ways. Some examples:

  • openscad porting work: http://github.com/markvdb/diybookscanner/openscad (in progress, helping hands welcome!)
  • an experiment with aluminium L profiles as brackets for the glass
  • documenting Canon A2200 ptp triggering: http://github.com/markvdb/diybookscanner/misc/settings.md
